How your team's answers stay private

Aggregate only. You see the team aggregate, never an individual response, and there is no drill-down.
Minimum of 5. No score appears until at least 5 people have answered.
No live count. While a cycle is open, no one sees a running tally of who has answered.
Subscription as privacy. The subscription pays for the aggregate; the business never exposes an individual.

This is the same promise your team sees before they answer (what your team sees) and on the public Security and privacy page.

What you and the owner can see

+The team wellbeing score and its trend
+Participation as a share of the team

What no one can see

xAny individual response, name, or who answered
xAny drill-down to a person
xAny sub-group below 5
Nothing on this page is a setting. The boundary is built into how Brio works.
