Questions

The body of the check-in: the employee answers the 1 to 3 questions the operator configured, in under 30 seconds, with quick emoji or slider taps and nothing to type. This is the employee-side render of 5.2 Check-in setup (the same curated questions and answer types). One question per screen, a progress indicator, then Submit. A failed submit goes to 10.6 with answers preserved, never a dead-end. For Yemi the employee. Serves J4 and item 3; on flow F2. Private token-gated surface, so noindex. Source of truth: ia/docs/pages/checkin-questions.md.

Mobile-first priority

Content blocks

One question per screen keeps each step tiny and each answer considered (Principle 5, low effort). The privacy promise is not repeated here (it is 10.1); only a small persistent "Anonymous" anchor remains, so trust carries through every question.

  1. Minimal top bar with an anonymity anchor: the Brio mark and the company name, plus a small "Anonymous" reminder.
  2. Progress: "Question 1 of 3" (or dots), so effort is bounded and the end is visible (J4). A one-question free-tier check-in shows a single step.
  3. The question: one question per screen, its text the focus.
  4. The answer input: emoji scale or slider, whichever the operator chose in 5.2. Quick taps, nothing to type.
  5. Move on: Next to the following question, Back to the previous one; on the last question the primary becomes Submit.

Components used: the 10.0 / 10.1 check-in chrome (no nav, no account). The emoji scale and the slider are thin page-specific controls; their types come from 0.6 Canonical lists and mirror 5.2. No new global is invented; the privacy content itself lives at 10.1.

Behavior

States and primary CTA

StateWhat showsAction
Question (emoji)the question, a row of emoji options, progressNext (or Submit on the last)
Question (slider)the question, a slider with end labels and a live valueNext (or Submit)
One-question (free tier)a single question, no multi-step progressSubmit directly
In progressprogress advances; Back returns without losing the answerBack / Next
Submitting (loading)a brief sending state, no double submit (F2 Submit)route on result
Submit error-to 10.6 Submit error, answers preserved (never a dead-end)
Success-to 10.3 Check-in complete

Primary CTA: Next while there are more questions; Submit on the last (or only) question, to 10.3 on success and 10.6 on failure. Back is the in-flow secondary. No account step and no other navigation, by design.

Private token-gated surface

SEO block (noindex)

Questions is a private, token-gated employee surface. It is not indexed and carries no schema. It is a transactional zone, per the platform SEO stance.

robotsnoindex, nofollow (token-gated, personal)
canonicalnone for search; flow URL is /c/{invite-token}
schema.orgnone
hreflang / OGnone

In-app findability (not SEO, still specified)

  • exactly one H1 per screen (the current question) for the accessible document outline
  • the emoji options and the slider are labelled form controls with a group label, never carried by color alone
  • the progress is a live region ("Question 2 of 3"); Next, Back, and Submit are labelled controls
  • tap targets at least 44px; the flow works without an account
