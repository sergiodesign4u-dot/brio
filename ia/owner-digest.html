Monthly owner digest

The owner's monthly touch, delivered. The email carries the month's score, the trend, and one honest sentence, so the owner gets the value in their inbox in under two minutes, with a single link to open the full dashboard (9.0). It is the retention loop for the owner (W1) and a quiet reminder the tool is working (J5, S2). Aggregate-only, never individual. Email is not crawled; the view-in-browser is a private token-gated page. This node closes cluster 9. Source of truth: ia/docs/pages/owner-digest.md.

Mobile-first priority

Content blocks

Block 1 is the first screen on a phone, where most email is read. The email delivers the value on its own; the link is the path to the full read.

  1. Email header: the sender and a plain subject ("Your Acme team wellbeing, June").
  2. The month's read: the score and one honest sentence. The value, delivered.
  3. Trend: the direction versus recent months, at a glance.
  4. Participation: as a share of the team, never who.
  5. Condensed privacy line: one 0.3 line ("you only ever see the team aggregate"), so the promise travels with the email.
  6. Primary action: "Open your dashboard" to 9.0, the single link.
  7. Email footer: sent monthly, manage frequency or unsubscribe, view in browser.

Components used: an email template layout (distinct from the app chrome), the pulse read reused from 9.0, a condensed 0.3 privacy line, and one primary link to 9.0. No new global; the email delivers the same aggregate-only read.

Email findability (not SEO, still specified)

  • a meaningful subject line and preview text; a plain-text alternative
  • semantic headings in the HTML email; the score, trend, and participation are text, not an image
  • participation is stated as a share, never as who; a missing score states its reason in text
  • table-based layout with inline styles, readable in light and dark clients, no meaning by color alone; tap targets at least 44px
